Important Changes to DuckDuckHack and the Newsletter

Hello everyone!

We have published an important update on the DuckDuckHack homepage notifying our community that DuckDuckHack is now in maintenance mode.

As part of these changes, we have also decided to discontinue the DuckDuckHack Newsletter until further notice.

On behalf of the DuckDuckGo Team we would like to sincerely thank all the contributors and Community Leaders who have helped DuckDuckHack move forward over these past years.

For more details, please visit https://duckduckhack.com

Sincerely,

- The DuckDuckGo Staff
